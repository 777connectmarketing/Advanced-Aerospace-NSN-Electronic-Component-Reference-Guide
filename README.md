# Section 1: Understanding the Importance of Aerospace-Grade Components

Aviation systems operate in environments with:

- Rapid temperature fluctuations  
- Extreme vibration  
- Fluid pressure cycles  
- Electromagnetic interference  
- Electrostatic discharge  
- Mechanical load stress  

Because of this, aircraft components must be:

- Traceable  
- Certified  
- Manufactured to MIL-SPEC or aerospace standards  
- Resistant to corrosion, heat, and fatigue  
- Designed for high reliability over thousands of hours
